Welcome

Welcome is a program of events that showcases all that life at UWE Bristol has to offer. It is jam-packed with opportunities to meet new people and make new friends. Our events and activities are for everyone: whether you are fresh out of school, coming to us as a mature student, or returning as a second or third year. This site gives you an idea about what to expect, we will be releasing more information about events and tickets over the summer so keep your eyes peeled and sign up with your email for updates.

Freshers Fair

Join in and find out about everything The Students’ Union, Sport Clubs, Societies, the university and Bristol itself has to offer. With over 200 stalls there is plenty of things to do, people to meet and sweets to eat.
